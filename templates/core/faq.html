Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, returns, and more.

Ordering & Payment

Placing an order is simple! Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll be redirected to WhatsApp to complete your order with our team. We'll confirm product availability, calculate delivery costs, and arrange payment.

We accept multiple payment methods including:

  • M-Pesa (Mobile Money)
  • Bank transfers
  • Cash on delivery (within Nairobi)
  • Visa and Mastercard (through secure payment gateways)

Payment details will be confirmed during your WhatsApp conversation with our team.

Yes, you can modify or cancel your order before it's processed and shipped. Contact us immediately via WhatsApp at +254 790420843. Once an order is shipped, modifications are not possible, but you can return items according to our return policy.

Shipping & Delivery

We deliver across Kenya with special focus on:

  • Nairobi: Same-day or next-day delivery
  • Major towns: Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret (2-3 days)
  • Other areas: 3-7 days depending on location

Delivery costs vary by location and will be calculated during order confirmation.

Shipping costs depend on your location and order size:

  • Within Nairobi: KES 200-500
  • Major towns: KES 300-800
  • Remote areas: KES 500-1,500

Free delivery for orders above KES 10,000 within Nairobi!

Once your order is shipped, we'll provide you with tracking information via WhatsApp. You can track your package using the courier's tracking system. We also send updates at key delivery milestones.

Returns & Exchanges

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be:

  • In original condition and packaging
  • Unused and undamaged
  • With all original tags and accessories

Contact us within 7 days to initiate a return. Return shipping costs may apply unless the item was damaged or incorrect.

To return an item:

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp at +254 790 420 843
  2. Provide your order details and reason for return
  3. We'll provide return instructions and address
  4. Package the item securely with original packaging
  5. Send the item back using our recommended courier

Refunds are processed within 3-7 business days after we receive the returned item.

If your item arrives damaged, contact us immediately via WhatsApp with photos of the damage. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund at no cost to you. We may also file a claim with the courier service.

Products & Quality

Yes, all our products are 100% authentic. We source directly from manufacturers and authorized distributors. Every product comes with a quality guarantee and authenticity certificate where applicable.

Many of our products come with manufacturer warranties. Warranty terms vary by product and are clearly stated on product pages. We also offer our own quality guarantee - if a product fails due to manufacturing defects within 30 days, we'll replace it free of charge.

We provide detailed product photos and descriptions on our website. For customers in Nairobi, we can arrange product viewing appointments at our office. We also offer video calls to show products in detail before purchase.
